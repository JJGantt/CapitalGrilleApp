## 1. Negroni Bianco

- Glass: Rocks Glass
- Garnish: Cucumber Ribbon

### Ingredients

- Drumshanbo Gunpowder Gin — 1 1/4 oz
- Salt-Washed Italicus — 1 oz
- Carpano Bianco Vermouth — 3/4 oz
- Large Ice Cube — 1 each
- Expressed Lemon Peel — 1 each

### Prep

1. Gently place large ice cube in center of rocks glass.
2. Add gin, Italicus, and vermouth to bar mixing glass.
3. Stir to combine drink for 10-15 seconds.
4. Strain into rocks glass.
5. Express a lemon peel on one side of the rim for essence, then discard.
6. Carefully place cucumber ribbon around ice cube as garnish.

## 2. Noble Paloma

- Glass: Highball Glass
- Garnish: Spicy Salt Rim, 1 Dehydrated Lime

### Ingredients

- Casa Noble Reposado — 1 1/2 oz
- Giffard Noix de Coco — 1/2 oz
- Lime Juice — 3/4 oz
- Fever Tree Grapefruit Soda — 4 1/2 oz

### Prep

1. Rim with spicy salt then add ice to highball glass.
2. Add Casa Noble Reposado, Giffard Noix de Coco, and lime juice to Boston shaker.
3. Add ice then shake vigorously to combine drink for 15-20 seconds.
4. Strain into highball glass.
5. Add Fever Tree Grapefruit Soda.
6. Lightly stir with bar spoon for 1-2 rotations to incorporate the grapefruit soda.
7. Garnish with dehydrated lime wedge.

## 3. Black Tie Margarita

- Glass: Black Stemless Wine Glass
- Garnish: Kosher Salt Half Rim, Lime Wedge

### Ingredients

- Calirosa Rosa Blanco Tequila — 1 1/2 oz
- Cointreau — 1/2 oz
- Lime Juice — 1 oz
- Lemon Juice — 1/2 oz
- Simple Syrup — 3/4 oz

### Prep

1. Salt half of the rim with kosher salt then add ice to black stemless wine glass.
2. Add tequila, Cointreau, lime juice, lemon juice, and simple syrup to Boston shaker.
3. Add ice then shake vigorously to combine drink for 15-20 seconds.
4. Strain into black stemless wine glass.
5. Garnish with lime wedge.

## 4. Blackberry Bourbon Sidecar

- Glass: Rocks Glass
- Garnish: 2 Blackberries on a TCG Pick, Sprig of Fresh Thyme

### Ingredients

- Blackberries — 3 each
- Angel's Envy Bourbon — 1 1/2 oz
- Cointreau — 1/4 oz
- Lemon Juice — 1/2 oz
- Simple Syrup — 1/2 oz

### Prep

1. Add ice to rocks glass.
2. Muddle 3 blackberries with simple syrup in Boston shaker.
3. Add Angel's Envy Bourbon, lemon juice, and Cointreau to Boston shaker.
4. Add ice then shake vigorously to combine drink for 15-20 seconds.
5. Double strain through a fine strainer into the rocks glass.
6. Garnish with 2 blackberries on a TCG pick and a fresh thyme sprig.

## 10. Passion Fruit Mojito

- Glass: Highball Glass
- Garnish: Mint Sprig

### Ingredients

- Mint Leaves — 8 each
- Real Passion Fruit Syrup — 1 1/2 oz
- Lime Juice — 3/4 oz
- Diplomatico Planas Rum — 1 1/2 oz
- Club Soda — 2 oz
- Diplomatico Reserva Exclusiva Rum — 1/2 oz

### Prep

1. Add ice to highball glass.
2. Add mint leaves, Real Passion Fruit Syrup, fresh lime juice, and Diplomatico Planas Rum to Boston shaker.
3. Add ice then shake vigorously to combine drink for 15-20 seconds.
4. Strain into highball glass.
5. Top with Club Soda.
6. Float Diplomatico Reserva Exclusiva Rum.
7. Garnish with a mint sprig.

# Wines by the Glass

## Sparkling & Rosé

### Riondo, Prosecco
- Tasting Notes: Persistent bubbles and slight hint of sweetness up front with a clean dry finish. Notes of lime, lemon zest, white flowers, and almond.
- Food Pairing: Seafood, fresh salads, & prosciutto

### Schramsberg, Mirabelle, Blanc de Blancs
- Tasting Notes: Chardonnay-based sparkling wine that is dry and crisp. Vibrant, fruitful and focused with bright aromas of Meyer lemon, golden apple and marzipan.
- Food Pairing: Oysters, shellfish, citrus-driven salads, & light fried dishes

### Moët & Chandon, Impérial, Champagne
- Tasting Notes: Aromas of green apple, citrus, and white flowers with hints of brioche and almond. Fresh and elegant, with creamy texture, crisp acidity, and a mineral finish.
- Food Pairing: Oysters, light poultry, and creamy & buttery dishes

### d'Esclans, Whispering Angel, Rosé
- Tasting Notes: Pretty pale pink color with notes of tart fresh fruit and spring flowers. In the mouth, the acidity is refreshing; a great elegance and a nice balance dominate, with a saline finish.
- Food Pairing: Seafood & fresh salads

## White Wine

### Hess 'Shirtail Ranches', Chardonnay
- Tasting Notes: Grilled pineapple, lemon zest, and a touch of oak. Balanced acidity and sweetness with flavors of peaches and golden delicious apples.
- Food Pairing: Roasted chicken & grilled seafood

### DeLille Cellars, Chaleur Blanc
- Tasting Notes: Offering hints of tropical fruit, peaches and lemon zest and on the palate, characteristic grapefruit and honeysuckle flavors.
- Food Pairing: Shellfish, roasted poultry, and fresh seafood flavors

### KB by Knight's Bridge, Chardonnay
- Tasting Notes: The nose is complex, forward and pure with hints of stone fruit, grilled almonds, Meyer lemon and toasted oak. On the palate the wine delivers the perfect balance of flavor, richness and structure.
- Food Pairing: Rich seafood, roasted chicken, & creamy or nut-accented dishes

### Diatom by Brewer-Clifton, Chardonnay
- Tasting Notes: Opens with aromas of lemon, citrus blossom, and wet sand. The palate is precise and balanced with flavors of saline and white peach.
- Food Pairing: Shellfish, tartare, simple grilled fish, and fresh salads

### Casa Lunardi, Pinot Grigio
- Tasting Notes: Light body and crisp with whispers of tropical fruit and crisp citrus notes. Its low aromatics and refreshing character make it ideal on its own as an aperitif or with seafood, shellfish and salads.
- Food Pairing: Seafood, shellfish, and fresh salads

### Santa Margherita, Pinot Grigio
- Tasting Notes: Medium bodied with a hint of sweetness (though a dry wine). This is a bolder, richer Pinot Grigio than the more straight forward Sartori.
- Food Pairing: Seafood, grilled chicken, and fresh salads

### SA Prüm, 'Essence', Riesling
- Tasting Notes: A nice low alcohol of 10–10.5% allows for a light easy drinking wine with just the right amount of sweetness to balance the crunchy slate-like minerality. Notes of fresh white flowers, white peach, and citrus zest dominate.
- Food Pairing: Spicy dishes, shellfish, and fresh salads

### Peter Yealands, Sauvignon Blanc
- Tasting Notes: The New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc is noted for its tropical fruits and intense citrus characteristics.
- Food Pairing: Salads, poultry, raw oysters, and delicate seafood
